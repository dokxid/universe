# Privacy Summary

The **CIE Universe** and its **Heritage Labs** are operated by the **Centre for International Heritage Activities (CIE)** in Delft, The Netherlands.
We collect only essential personal data required for user accounts, platform operation, and communication.
Data are processed in accordance with the **EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)** and are not shared for commercial purposes.

For details on how we handle your information and your rights under GDPR, please see our full **[Privacy Policy](#privacy-policy--cie-universe-and-heritage-labs)**.
