## 2. Purpose and Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how the **CIE Universe** and its associated **Heritage Labs** collect, use, and protect personal data in connection with the operation of the platform at [https://app.heritagelab.center/](https://app.heritagelab.center/).

The policy applies to all users, including **Superadmins**, **Admins**, and **Heritage Lab Members**, and to all visitors of the website.

---

## 3. Data We Collect

We only collect personal data that are necessary for the operation and security of the platform. This may include:

* **Account data:** name, email address, institutional affiliation, profile information.
* **Content data:** texts, images, 3D models, and metadata uploaded to Heritage Labs.
* **Technical data:** IP address, browser type, operating system, time of access, and similar server logs.
* **Communication data:** emails, messages, and feedback sent through the platform.
* **Cookies:** small data files used to maintain sessions and user preferences (see section 7).

We do **not** collect sensitive data (e.g., race, religion, or health information) unless explicitly provided for project documentation purposes and only with prior consent.

---

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases under the **EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):**

* **Article 6(1)(a) GDPR – Consent:** when you voluntarily provide information or agree to data processing (e.g., when registering a user account).
* **Article 6(1)(b) GDPR – Contract performance:** when data are necessary to provide access to the platform and its services.
* **Article 6(1)(f) GDPR – Legitimate interests:** for the secure, efficient, and lawful operation of the platform, including server maintenance, analytics, and fraud prevention.
* **Article 6(1)(c) GDPR – Legal obligation:** where we are required to retain or disclose data under applicable law.

---

## 5. Use of Data

Your personal data are used exclusively for:

* Creating and managing user accounts.
* Operating and maintaining the CIE Universe and its Heritage Labs.
* Managing uploaded content and related metadata.
* Responding to user requests and ensuring data security.
* Statistical analysis to improve functionality (anonymized where possible).
* Compliance with ethical, institutional, and legal obligations.

No automated decision-making or profiling is performed.

---

## 6. Data Sharing and Recipients

Your data may be shared with:

* Authorized platform administrators and Superadmins within the CIE Universe.
* Technical service providers (e.g., hosting, cloud storage, or maintenance partners) under strict data processing agreements.
* Partner institutions only when collaboration requires shared access and when proper safeguards are in place.

We do **not** sell, rent, or otherwise disclose personal data to third parties for commercial purposes.

---

## 7. Cookies and Tracking

This website uses **essential cookies** required for login and navigation.
No marketing or third-party tracking cookies are used.

If optional analytics tools (e.g., **Matomo**) are activated, users will be informed and given the option to opt out.
Analytics data are anonymized and stored within the EU.

---

## 8. Data Retention

We store personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes stated above or as required by law.

Inactive accounts and related content may be anonymized or deleted after **24 months** of inactivity, unless preservation is required for archival or research purposes under institutional policies.

Users may request deletion of their data at any time (see Section 10).

---

## 9. International Data Transfers

If data are processed or stored outside the **European Economic Area (EEA)**, this will only occur where adequate safeguards (such as **EU Standard Contractual Clauses**) are in place to protect your personal data.

---

## 10. Your Rights (Articles 12–22 GDPR)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

* **Access** – to know what data we hold about you.
* **Rectification** – to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
* **Erasure (“Right to be forgotten”)** – to request deletion of your data.
* **Restriction of processing** – to limit how your data are used.
* **Data portability** – to obtain a copy in a structured format.
* **Objection** – to processing based on legitimate interests.
* **Withdrawal of consent** – at any time, without affecting prior processing.

Requests may be sent to [info@heritage-activities.org](mailto:info@heritage-activities.org).
We will respond within one month as required by law.

---

## 11. Security Measures

We apply appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse, including:

* Encrypted connections (**HTTPS**).
* Role-based access control for admins and members.
* Regular system updates and backups.
* Data minimization and pseudonymization where feasible.

---

## 12. Hosting and Infrastructure

The **CIE Universe** platform is hosted on servers located within the **European Union**.
Hosting and technical support are provided by trusted service providers operating under **GDPR-compliant agreements**.
